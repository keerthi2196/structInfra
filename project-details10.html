HLB ACROSS KUNDARA VAGU

The HLB across Kundara Vagu would greatly enhance the connectivity in the region, bridging communities and economies on either side of the river and providing seamless transportation options.

The bridge will streamline the flow of goods and services, which can stimulate trade and contribute to local economic growth. It can also attract new businesses and industries to the area.

The construction of the HLB across Kundara Vagu will stand as an engineering marvel, showcasing modern construction techniques and sustainable practices, and symbolizing progress and development in the region.

The bridge will offer a safer and more reliable means of crossing the Kundara Vagu, particularly in adverse weather conditions. This can lead to a significant improvement in road safety in the region.

Project information

  • Client PANCHAYAT RAJ, Government of Andhra Pradesh-2021
